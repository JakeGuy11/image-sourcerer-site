Contribution Information

   We are looking for contributions of any kind - not just monetary. Below is a list of all the ways you personally can contribute to make Image Sourcerer better. Before that, though, it's important to address where your monetary contributions will go. Our first priority is buying a server and a domain. Once we do that, many other features will be unlocked for everyone, such as firefox support, automatic submission forms and much faster download times for Pixiv and some of reddit, plus much more. After that, the proceeds will go to speeding up the development of the extension. If you would like to specify where you want your contribution to go, reach out to us and we will accomodate you! All recurring donators will be notified when the first goal has been reached in case they would like to stop donating.

Spread the Word!

   This is by far the best way to contribute to the growth of Image Sourcerer! The more people that use it, the more excited we are to work on it for everyone! We really appreciate you telling your friends about Image Sourcerer if you enjoy it. We may be able to secure fundraising once the GitHub repository reaches 100 stars, so please star it if you have an account as well!

Donations

   Outside of telling other people about Image Sourcerer, donating is the best thing you can do. We will happily accept anything you're willing to give to reach our goals. That said, the website we accept donations through only allows you to donate on a regular basis - this can be avoided by cancelling your donations after the first one. If we can get just 10 people donating $3 per week, we will be able to afford the domain and server in under two months!

Contribute Code

   If you have coding experience and think you can contribute to the project, great! We're always happy to accept help. Head over to the Contact the Devs page and let us know what your skills are and what you think you can help us with!

Help Translate

   At this time, we have not officially started support for other languages, but we have plans to in the near future. If you speak a language other than english enough to help us translate it, please contact us, we would like to be accessable to as many people as possible!
